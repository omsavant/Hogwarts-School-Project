Hogwarts School Of Witchcraft and Wizardary


This school of Witchcraft and Wizardary is located in Scotland beside Hogsmead Village. This school mainly teaches 7 subjects :
1. Transfiguration
2. Potions
3. Charms
4. Herbology
5. History of Magic
6. Astronomy
7. Defence Against the Dark Arts.
This school has four houses:
1. Gryffindor
2. Hufflepuff
3. Ravenclaw
4. Sltherin

Great Hall


This place is where the students eat and attend their O.W.Ls and N.E.T.Ws.

Hogwarts has many formalities. It has a place where you can eat, attend your examinations etc. There are dormitories for each house. This school will provide medical facilities, food, water, beds etc. Children have to bring a wand, quills, a pet (optional toad, owl or rat), pachment paper, books of the specific year (if 1st year all subject books are compulsary if not you can choose the subjects) All lessons except Astronomy will be held in day. Students have to go to Platform 9 and 3 quarters to go to Hogwarts. Students will first be sorted into the four houses. This school is only for witches and wizards (not non-magical muggles). The house cup for winning, the houses must not break rules or points will be taken and points wil be gained by answering questions (only if the answer is right). The third years if want to visit Hogsmead Village, a parent or guardian should sign a form given. The rule breaking may or may not land the student into detension. There is a common room and boys and girls dormitories. The beds are in the boys and girls dormitories. The subject of Astronomy will be taken on a tower for better watching the sky. To enter the dormitories there may be obstacles like you have to say a password gor entering the Gryffindor Common room or you have to answer a riddle to enter Ravenclaw common room. The do's and don'ts wil be told at the starting of the year. This school has 7 years. It startes at the age of 11 if the child will show any magical signs. In the first year you will have easy and simple spells and as you go to higher years you will have more complicated spells. Dark arts will not be tolerated in this school.

Achievements


Quiddich

Very good game for the magical world

Quiddich is a game for the magical world. There are 7 players in Quiddich:
1. Three chasers
2. Two Beaters
3. A Keeper
4. A seeker.
Each of these players have different roles in the game. There are 3 types of balls in Quiddich:
1. A Quaffle
2. Two Bludgers
3. A Snitch.
The different types of players have different roles:
1. The Chasers pass the Quaffle to each other and try to throw it through the three hoops. By doing so, it earns them 10 points.
2. The Beaters are the only players allowed to utilise their bats. They hit the Bludgers with these bats to dismantle players of the opponents' team. The opponets' Beaters also do this for the same intension.
3. The Keeper defends the hoops from the opponents' Chasers from scoring goals.
4. The Seeker's only job is to catch the Golden Snich. This ball is not to be used by other players of the team or the opponents players except their Seeker. If the Seeker of a team catches the Snich, the game ends and the team in which the Seeker belongs to, gets 160 points.


The Black Lake
A very big lake. Good for putting legs in. Hard to cross physically.

A large, freshwater lake (a loch, actually, since this is Scotland) lies to the south of Hogwarts castle, which stands on a cliff overlooking the water. The lake has a number of very unusual inhabitants, including a giant squid (a semi-domesticated squid as it lets students to tickle it's tentacles while basking in the sun in shallow waters), a village of merfolk, and many grindylows. The lake is approximately a half-mile in diameter. The lake is also known as the Black Lake. Every school year at Hogwarts, first years sail across the lake to the castle led by a teacher in boats as per Hogwarts tradition. When they get to the school, they are then Sorted into their houses. The Giant Squid also helps people if the are drowning in the Black Lake where The Giant Squid lives in.
